ABOUT US

Uzbekistan Textile Industry

The range of our activities includes establishing and expanding relationships with other nations in the fields of textile and sewing production, organizing and holding forums, conferences, seminars, round tables, training sessions, and exhibitions with participation of international organizations, major brands and retailers, as well as foreign experts, specialists, and speakers for business representatives. Additionally, the association is working to expand the practice of introducing international standards at enterprises, and organizes training for their employees in the field of standardization and certification.
