About Us

The Association of Textile and Clothing and Knitting Industry Enterprises of Uzbekistan “Uztekstilprom” was established by Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated December 14, 2017 No. UP-5285. In accordance with it, the volume and quality of textile products are increasing, and its range is also expanding. The best design developments are introduced into the production process based on the trends and directions of the modern fashion industry

For Society

An organization of top producers of textile, apparel, and knitwear products in Uzbekistan that is constantly growing, solving social problems, giving people a place to work, and creating new jobs. Member enterprises whose goods are utilized on a daily basis by millions of families across the globe.
