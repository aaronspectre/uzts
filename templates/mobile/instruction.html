How to become a member of the association

Documents that must be submitted for admission of the enterprise to full members of the Association in accordance with paragraph 3 of the Procedure approved by the Minutes of the meeting of the Uztekstilprom Association dated April 19, 2019.

List:
  • Letter of application from the organization;
  • Enterprise passport in the form of a sample;
  • Certificate of state registration of the organization, a copy of the constituent documents (with all changes and additions);
  • A copy of the balance sheet for the last reporting period;
  • Detailed information about the installed equipment (in the form of an attached sample);
  • A copy of the decision or order on the appointment of the head of the organization, a certificate of employment;
  • A copy of the business plan for the production of the enterprise for at least one year;
  • Separate information on products imported by the organization on the basis of customs preferences (in the form of an attached sample);
  • Decision of the founder to join the association, written consent (minutes);
  • Production of photographic materials (6-10 pieces), products and the appearance of the building;
  • A copy of the lease agreement if the building is being used on a lease basis;
  • A copy of the cadastral document when the building belongs to the enterprise. (corresponding to 2-3 sheets).

Memo

  • The appeal is addressed to the Chairman of the Association "Uztekstilprom" - Khaydarov I.U.
  • Constituent documents - certificate, articles of association and memorandum of association.
  • Balance - refers to the balance sheet approved by the State Tax Committee.
