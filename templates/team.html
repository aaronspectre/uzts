Ilkhom Khaydarov

Chairman of the Textile and Garment Industry Association “Uztextileprom”

Uzbekistan as having a long history in weaving artisanal textiles, or you might know our country as one that produces cotton but I would like further to introduce you to a modern Uzbekistan, with a state-of-the-art textile industry and a value chain that runs from the production of the fiber to manufacturing of the finished product. An industry that has grown several folds in the past few years to reach by the end of 2022 US $ 3,5 Billion in exports with a fully transparent manufacturing chain capable of converting 100% of our local production of fiber.

I look forward to seeing you in Uzbekistan and providing you with all the support you need to make this your new and preferred sourcing destination. Uztextileprom and myself stand as your partners to make your business more successful.
